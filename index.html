Sources & official texts

This app is a study aid. Where the answer quotes a confessional standard it is labeled (e.g. Westminster Shorter Catechism); text marked Study note is the author's gloss — confirm it against the official texts below. Reference chips on each card link out to the source.

  • Book of Church Order (PCA) — official current BCO (pcaac.org) · browsable (pcahistory.org)
  • Westminster Confession of Faith — with proof texts (opc.org)
  • Westminster Larger Catechism — opc.org
  • Westminster Shorter Catechism — opc.org
  • Exam preparation guide — Chapell/Meek, Preparing for Licensure & Ordination Exams (the standard weekly-schedule companion)

Note on provenance: the BCO is copyrighted, so all BCO content here is paraphrase — the original Q&A deck derives from a third-party 2007 study set, and the governance sub-decks are modern paraphrases keyed to current BCO chapter numbers. Always verify wording and numbering against the official BCO (linked above). The Westminster Standards content is quoted from the public-domain text. Exams are set per presbytery; there is no standard PCA question bank.
